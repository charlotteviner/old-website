About Me

I am a 22-year-old postgraduate student at the University of Leeds studying for a MSc in River Basin Dynamics and Management with Geographical Information Systems. Prior to joining the University of Leeds, I studied for a BSc in Environmental Geoscience at the University of Nottingham, graduating with a first class honours degree.

Primarily, my academic interests focus around hydrology. I am particularly interested in river geomorphology, and this formed the basis of my undergraduate dissertation. Over the past year, I have become increasingly interested in developing my GIS skills and in learning new skills, such as programming using Python.

This website is my online portfolio of work undertaken as a part of my academic studies. More specifically, it is currently a portfolio of projects I have completed in Python. I have mainly focused on agent-based models, one created during guided practical sessions for one of my Master's modules, and the other created independently as part of an extended project.
