Core Skills: Individual Project

For my individual project, I created an agent-based model which simulates rain moving within a landscape. The model creates raindrops as agents, moves them downslope in the landscape, and allows the user to map the drainage network of the landscape and calculate the total volume of water that has reached an outlet point in the landscape.
